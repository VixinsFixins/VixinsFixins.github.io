ğŸ§­Directions

  1. Combine panko, cheese, and smoked paprika-garlic blend in a shallow dish. Preheat oven to 425F.
  2. Pat chicken dry with paper towel.
  3. Carefully slice into the centre of each chicken breast lengthwise and parallel to the cutting board - leaving 1/2 inch intact on the other end.
  4. Open up chicken like a book, and season both sides with salt and pepper and coat one side of each chicken breast with mayo.
  5. Firmly press mayo-coated side into panko mixture to adhere, one at a time.
  6. Transfer chicken to a parchment-lined baking sheet, coated-side up. Bake chicken on the middle oven rack until cooked through (18-20 mins).
